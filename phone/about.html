ABOUT US

 

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

 

IEEE RGIT's mission is the all round development of its members in not just good leaders and engineers but also better human beings!

 

IEEE-RGIT has a history of flooding its events with unique clarity and with a way of expressing every students mind from racing into creativity along with the technical passion hand in hand. As an IEEE member, you are part of the world’s largest community of technology professionals—a network of communication and learning where innovators share what works, what does not, and what is needed now.

 

ABOUT RGIT

 

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology is a certified premier educational institution established under Manjara Charitable Trust in 1992. It is Affiliated and approved by the Mumbai University along with the All India Council for Technical Education (A.I.C.T.E). This institute has created recorded annals in these passing years, which has awarded this institute with an ”A” grade.
