About

 

From the past 10 years, our members of IEEE-RGIT have created informative and exciting events for the students to enrich their ways of using technology.  We have encouraged innovative ideas and have held workshops on modern technology which met the industry requirements.

 

 

We believe that softwares have become an integral part of our technology. Today, almost all modern appliances are software oriented. We conduct various workshops throughout the year to encourage knowledge sharing on several types of applications. This helps our students to be pro at the latest happenings of the industry.

 

 

Our branch has been honored several times to conduct seminars headed by famous personalities who have motivated and inspired our students by sharing their experiences and igniting the "self belief" amongst many.
